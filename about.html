KEEP THE LIFE

Feeling stressful and losing-control when doing daily events recording? Always have a long list of to-dos but just can't cross even half of it? Here is the app coming to help.
It is not an app that asks you to record events and set to-do based on date. Instead, this app asks you to record events by goal. You may first have a overall goal, for example, you may want to finish reading a book. Then you can create a clip for this goal, which can be understood as the to-do list only for this event: finish reading a book. You can also add a time limit for your goal, such as a week, or from July to September. It's all up to you!
