Project Proposal

Topic: Everyday Life in Quarantine

Problem/Data Domain
  1. Overview of the General Topic
    The pandemic has changed everyone's life, and one of the most significant ways it changes people's life is through quarantine. Because of the existence of pandemic, people are not able to go out and get in touch with other human beings like before. Instead, they have to stay at home to avoid as much as contacting with other people as they could. In fact, many things that people have worried about are actually not a problem for nowadays. People can talk with their friends through message application or video talking applicaiton. There are many application like instacart that helps people with shopping grocery stuff. Netflix is definition a good choice for dealing with boring time... As for me, the biggest problem is, without the constraints from other people and the general public, how can people manage their life in a regular mode? For example, for college students like me, befroe quarantine, even though sometimes we were just too lazy to get up to go to a class, or just too tired to start doing an assignment or reviewing, but if this class had no recording, or this class had participation point, or our roomates had already got up, or the studying atmosphere of the library... There were always so many factors from the physical boundary that we were living in--college--forced us to get up and do right things. However, during quarantine, everybody is like being isolated in an island. We are no longer have that close relationship with our surrouding environemnt. At this time, it gradually becomes a big problem for many to manage their life. How to live like on college campus when you are actually in your comfort home?

  3. Previous or Existing Attempts
    Those apps that are aiming for recording daily life, no matter in the form of video recording or traditional writing, could be identified as existing attempts to help with this topics. As by recording their life everyday, people are better able to realize what life they are living everyday and implementing changes.

App Description
  1. Who are the users of the application?
    People in quarantine that are not able to manage their life well.
  2. What kind of information will the users be viewing?
    Other people's recording life. In other words, the way other people are using to manage their life during quarantine. In conclusion, this app would offer a place for user to record their everyday of quarantine life, set goals and plan for quarantine life. And they can not only recording their own life, but also viewing other users' recording and hope to also live a normal life during quarantine.
  3. What will the users do with the web app? What kind of interactons are your expecting to provides?
    The user will be able to record their everyday life on the app. It will not be like writing a long blog as long paragraph is hard for people to focus on. Instead, the app will be like a questionaire that asks the user many questions everyday. Users can design their own everyday questions or use the default questions the app offer. There will also be space for the user to DIY their everyday recording to make it as colorful and interesting as they want. This app is aiming for living good life in qurantine, so it will only focus on quarantine, not like other normal recording life application like doing diaries everyday, and doing vlog everyday, etc.
  4. How will using this app help to solve your chosen problem?
    I hope that by recroding their life on this app, the user can soon find their onw way to manage their life during quarantine. I hope by using this app, they could be able to find a living pattern that suits their livign habits and work/study/relax requirements and follow it regualrly everyday.
